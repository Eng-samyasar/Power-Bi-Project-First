# 🚀 My First Power BI Project

I just completed my first **Power BI** project, transforming a single flat table into a **Star Schema with 5 related tables**.

## 📊 Key Insights from the Data

### 🥇 Top US State
**California** – $2.4M  
Mostly driven by **Office Supplies**

### 📉 Lowest US States
**New Mexico & Virginia** – Around $1K each

### 🏆 Top Category
**Arts & Crafts** → $111K profit

### 📉 Lowest Category
**Telephony** → $191 profit

### 📅 Monthly Profit Peaks
- February 2020  
- November 2021  
- March 2022  

### 🌐 Sales Channels
**Online sales lead slightly with 54% of total profit**
